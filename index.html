    Focus on Grammar

    First Conditional

    ⭐ The First Conditional — future possibility

    We use the first conditional to talk about things that are possible in the future.

    IF + present simple,will / won't + infinitive

    💡 Example : If they go back to Pakistan, Malala's life will be in danger.

    📖 Exercise 1 — True or False Section 1

    1
    Malala comes from Lahore?
    FALSE
    They come from Mingora, in the Swat region of Pakistan.
    2
    Taliban banned girls' education in Swat in 2009?
    TRUE
    Stated in the first paragraph of the article.
    3
    Malala started her blog at age 14?
    FALSE
    She was only 11 years old when she started writing for the BBC.
    4
    Taliban shot Malala on 9th October 2012?
    TRUE
    Tuesday 9th October 2012 — stated in the introduction.
    5
    Malala went to hospital in France?
    FALSE
    She went to hospital in Pakistan first, then flew to the UK for operations.
    6
    Malala went back to school on 19th March 2013 in Birmingham?
    TRUE
    Correct — she started at an all-girls school in Birmingham, England.
    7
    Kainat can take the bus to school?
    FALSE
    Buses and taxis refuse to take her, so she studies from home.

    ❓ Exercise 2 — Open questions Section 1

    1
    Why did the Taliban shoot them?
    The Taliban shot them because the girls went to school. They wanted to stop girls from getting an education.
    2
    What did Malala write about?
    She wrote about the rights of girls — the right to education, to play, to sing, and to speak up.
    3
    What was the unexpected result?
    Instead of silencing her, the Taliban made her voice famous all over the world.
    4
    Why doesn't Kainat go to school?
    Buses and taxis refuse to take her to school, so she has to study from home.

    📰 Exemple d'article modèle Section 5

    A Brave Elephant Saves Lives
    By A. Student | 19th March 2013

    On 26th December 2004, a young girl named Kris was riding her elephant, Hannah, on a beach in Thailand. Suddenly, Hannah stopped and refused to move. She ran away from the sea and carried Kris to safety on higher ground. A few minutes later, a huge tsunami hit the beach. Hannah's actions saved Kris and many other people. Kris's family did not understand what happened at first. However, they were very grateful for Hannah's courage. If Hannah had not acted so quickly, many more lives would have been lost.

    ✅ Cet exemple contient : passé simple, description claire, première conditionnelle en fin d'article.
